1078The James Beard Foundation is committed to a vibrant, equitable, and sustainable independent restaurant industry where all have the opportunity to thrive. To achieve our vision, we live and breathe our brand promises every day.</h4>

10831. We are champions of the independent restaurant industry in America</h5>

1084<p class="p light">

1085We recognize the unique value and power of the independent restaurant community and we devote our energy, resource and focus to promoting, protecting and enhancing that value cultural, societal and economic. We understand the challenges that our community face and dedicate ourselves to finding solutions to overcome them.</p>

1088</p>

10902. We balance celebration and impact</h5>

1091<p class="p light">

1092We are the only nonprofit in the food arena that rejoices in the delight and deliciousness of food and the precious nature of food culture that leads chefs and the broader culinary industry towards a new standard of excellence.</p>

1095</p>

10973. We are outcome-focused and purpose-driven</h5>

1130Chef and operators are our change agents. We recognize their unique position of influence and agency. Through our Awards and events, we spotlight those leading the way; and through our training and education programs we equip our community to push for a new standard of excellence in independent restaurants and for a better food system.</p>

11355. We partner with chefs to harness the power of food to drive positive change in the world</h5>

1137We are committed to excellence. Over the decades, we have actively evolved our definition of excellence, always centered in talent and deliciousness, and reflecting food culture and practices. We hold ourselves to the highest standard, and excellence runs through everything we do.</p>

11426. We guide and inspire food lovers</h5>

1143<p class="p light">

1144Diners and food lovers are essential for a thriving independent restaurant industry. Through our Awards and events, we help broaden their horizons, steer them towards new culinary experiences, and keep them in the know. It is also our job to inform food lovers of the critical value of independent restaurants, to foster a deep appreciation for the industrys vital contributions to American culture, and to educate them on their role in supporting the survival and vibrancy of the industry.</p>
